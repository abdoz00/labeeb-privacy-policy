## Privacy Policy

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Account Information
- **Email address** (required for Firebase Authentication)
- **User ID** (generated by Firebase Authentication)
- **Profile information** (optional display name, profile photo)

#### 1.2 Task Data
- **Task titles and descriptions** (stored in Firebase Firestore)
- **Task due dates, reminders, and notifications** (stored in Firebase Firestore)
- **Task completion status and XP/achievement data** (stored in Firebase Firestore)
- **AI parsing requests** (sent to Google Gemini API for natural language processing)
- **Voice and Audio Data:** When using the voice-to-task feature, the app records your audio to transcribe it into text via Google Gemini API. We do not store or retain your voice recordings on our servers after the transcription process is complete.

#### 1.3 Analytics and Crash Data
- **App usage statistics** (collected via Firebase Analytics)
- **Crash reports** (collected via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Performance metrics** (collected via Firebase Performance Monitoring)

#### 1.4 Advertising Data
- **Advertising identifier** (for ad personalization via Google AdMob)
- **Ad engagement data** (collected by Google AdMob)

#### 1.5 Subscription and Purchase Data
- **Purchase history** (processed via RevenueCat and Google Play)
- **Subscription status** (managed via RevenueCat)

### 2. How We Use Your Information

#### 2.1 Core App Functionality
- To authenticate your account and provide access to your tasks
- To sync your tasks across devices via Firebase Firestore
- To send push notifications for task reminders
- To provide AI-powered task parsing via Google Gemini API
- To track your progress, XP, and achievements

#### 2.2 Analytics and Improvement
- To analyze app usage patterns and improve user experience
- To diagnose and fix crashes via Firebase Crashlytics
- To monitor app performance

#### 2.3 Advertising
- To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
- To personalize ad experience based on your interests

#### 2.4 Subscription Management
- To process in-app purchases and subscriptions via RevenueCat
- To manage subscription status and entitlements

### 3. Data Storage and Security

#### 3.1 Data Storage
- **User account data:** Stored in Firebase Authentication (Google servers)
- **Task data:** Stored in Firebase Firestore (Google servers)
- **Analytics data:** Stored in Firebase Analytics (Google servers)
- **Crash reports:** Stored in Firebase Crashlytics (Google servers)

#### 3.2 Data Security
- All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Firebase provides industry-standard security measures
- We do not store your data on our own servers
- Firebase access is controlled via Firebase Security Rules

#### 3.3 Data Retention
- Task data is retained until you delete your account
- Analytics and crash data is retained according to Firebase's policies
- You can request deletion of your data at any time
